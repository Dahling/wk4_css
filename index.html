About Jenna

Hello, dahling. My name is Jenna Cooper, and I'm happy you've stopped by. To say that I "do" a particular thing would be misleading, as I have done many. Overall, I consider myself to be very well-rounded, when it comes to my educational background and work experience. After graduating with a degree in psychology and a minor in journalism, I decided to pursue a lifelong dream of acting in the City of Angels. In addition to acting, I have always had a great love for writing; and, I am gradually writing a book in creative non-fiction.

Career-wise, I have chosen to explore many arenas, which has led me to be the person I am, today. I have dabbled into the roles of: actress, waitress, hostess, office assistant, assistant to the VP of finance, sales associate at a multitude of high-end retailers (e.g., Nordstrom, GUESS?, Marciano, LF Boutiques, Tiffany, Neiman Marcus), preschool teacher, promotional model (e.g., brand ambassador), and flight attendant. My background has enabled me to become comfortable and confident, when it comes to connecting to people with a wide-range of personalities. I have a great love for people, and one of my top strengths is empathy.

Due to my great love of writing, I began a blog that I created and wrote on for over 3.5 years. During that time (circa 2010), I began teaching myself about CSS, html, blogging platforms, GoDaddy, domain names, Wordpress, and the likes. Ever since, during spare time, I am almost always on my MacBook, exploring the world inside a computer. Ultimately, this is what led me to Epicodus and the CSS/Design Track. Aside from my love of blogging and the internet, I have been known as an all-too-curious person to those whom I am closest to. That said, I chose to embark upon the world of coding. ...Stepping inside a whole new world is the least bit "new" to me; nevertheless, I have immense admiration for every single one of my peers, as I can most definitely say this isn't a world in which fear can thrive.
